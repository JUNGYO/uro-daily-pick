Precision Medicine
Molecular characterization and prognostic biomarkers in prostate, kidney, and bladder cancers. NGS, liquid biopsy, single CTC isolation, multi-institutional cohort studies. Causal AI models for personalized adjuvant therapy selection in renal cell carcinoma.

Validated ML models for prostate biopsy recommendation, surgical risk stratification, and postoperative outcome prediction. Multimodal and explainable AI designed for real clinical workflows — built to earn a clinician's trust.

AI Safety
Secure AI · Red Team
Homomorphic encryption enabling multi-institutional analysis without raw data exposure. LLM red-teaming and prompt injection vulnerability research in clinical settings (JAMA Network Open 2025–26). Defining what trustworthy medical AI must be able to withstand.
